# Comprehensive Uzbekistan Herpetofauna Database - Summary

## Database Overview

This comprehensive database now contains **62 species** of amphibians and reptiles documented from Uzbekistan.

## AMPHIBIANS (7 species)

### Order Caudata - Salamanders (1 species)
1. **Hynobius turkestanicus** - Turkestanian Salamander ⚠️ ENDANGERED
   - Endemic to Central Asian mountains
   - High elevation species (1500-3000m)
   - Red Book of Uzbekistan

### Order Anura - Frogs and Toads (6 species)

**Family Bufonidae - Toads (4 species)**
2. **Bufotes viridis** - Green Toad
   - Most common and widespread toad
   - Urban adapter
3. **Bufotes pewzowi** - Pewzow's Toad
   - Eastern Uzbekistan, Fergana Valley
4. **Bufotes perrini** - Perrin's Green Toad
   - Southern Uzbekistan
5. **Bufotes sitibundus** - Turan Toad
   - Southern regions

**Family Ranidae - True Frogs (2 species)**
6. **Pelophylax ridibundus** - Marsh Frog
   - Most common aquatic frog
   - Throughout Uzbekistan near water
7. **Rana asiatica** - Central Asian Frog
   - Mountain species
